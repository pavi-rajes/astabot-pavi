## Architecture

GatedDynamicRoutingRNN: GRU-style gated RNN (N=100).
- gate = sigmoid(W_gate @ y + W_gate_in @ u + b_gate)
- y_new = gate * y + (1-gate) * relu(W_rec @ y + W_in @ u)
- Gate initialized so gate≈0.2 (matching vanilla RNN alpha)
- hint_strength fades 1.0 → 0 over 2000 Stage 3 steps (2× longer than failed v25)

## Key hypothesis

Teacher forcing gives the gate a gradient signal to learn from. Once the gate learns
to hold context (gate → 1 for context units), the hint can fade without collapse.
The 88% signal retention test (vs 0.8% vanilla) confirmed the capacity exists.

## Success criterion

d'_inter > 0 after hint_strength < 0.1 (step ~1800+) would confirm the gate learned.
d'_inter > 1.5 for 2 consecutive evals = Stage 3 advancement.
